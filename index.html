MassBrowser

MassBrowser is a state-of-the-art volunteer-based censorship circumvention tool developed at the SPIN Lab at UMass, Amherst. MassBrowser enables users in censored countries to freely browse the Web with the help of volunteers from countries with open access to the Internet. MassBrowser functions with a lower maintainance cost compared to traditional circumvention tools and provides robustness to being blocked by censoring countries.
